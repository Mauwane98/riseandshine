About Rise and Shine Chess Club

Founded in September 2024 in Nellmapius, Pretoria, Rise and Shine Chess Club is a registered development club under Tshwane Chess. We are passionate about using the game of chess as a tool for intellectual, personal, and social development.

Our Mission

To foster a vibrant and inclusive community where individuals of all ages and skill levels can discover, learn, and grow through the game of chess. We aim to cultivate critical thinking, sportsmanship, and perseverance, empowering our members to excel both on and off the chessboard.

Our Vision

To be a leading hub for chess enthusiasm and development in our community, recognized for promoting intellectual growth, fostering strong social connections, and inspiring a lifelong passion for the game. We envision a future where the strategic lessons of chess empower every member to approach challenges with confidence and creativity.

What Makes Us Unique

  • Dedicated to youth and community development
  • Affiliated with Tshwane Chess regional body
  • Strong emphasis on inclusive participation
  • Volunteer-led with experienced chess mentors

Whether you're a young learner, a casual player, or an aspiring champion, Rise and Shine Chess Club welcomes you to be part of something great.
